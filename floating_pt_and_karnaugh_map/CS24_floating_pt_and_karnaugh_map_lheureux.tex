\textbf{3.} Give the equation (in Sum-Of-Products form) for this truth table,
then use a Karnaugh map to simplify. Show your table and final equation.

$$ \bar{A}BC + A\bar{B}C + ABC$$

\vspace{5mm}

\begin{center}
    \begin{karnaugh-map}[2][4][1][$C$][$AB$]
        \minterms{3,5,7}
        \maxterms{0,1,2,4,6}
        \implicant{3}{7}
        \implicant{7}{5}
    \end{karnaugh-map}    
\end{center}
\vspace{-0.125in}
$$\boxed{AC + BC}$$
